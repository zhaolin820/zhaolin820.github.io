I received the first B.Eng. degree from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, the second B.Eng. degree (Hons.) from the Queen Mary University of London, U.K., in 2020, and the M.Sc. degree (Distinction) from Imperial College London, U.K., in 2021. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Queen Mary University of London under the supervision of Dr. Yuanwei Liu. My current research interests include near-field communications, integrated sensing and communications, wideband systems, and optimization theory.
